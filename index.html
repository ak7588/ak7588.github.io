What could possibly happen when you go for an adventure on Halloween?

Right...almost anything! 👻 Mystery House is an award-winning movie by the world-famous 30 Minute Film Festival. Produced in Abu Dhabi and Dubai, the United Arab Emirates, the movie portrays a dramatic story of three friends — Amina, Ishmal, and Hessa — who are about to embark on their Halloween's Eve adventure when something mysterious happens in their house.

Are you ready to see what happens next?
